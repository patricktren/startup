About

GPT-Notes is a platform designed to enhance your experience using Chat-GPT.
Here you can create Notebooks with Folders and Pages; each Page can be used
as a whiteboard in which you can simply click anywhere and start querying!
Write out your query anywhere on the board and receive a response directly
from Chat-GPT immediately or just write a note that is relevant to your project!
Additionally, you can edit responses from Chat-GPT to your heart's desire to
only include the information that you want to reference later.
